We are on the brink of something truly revolutionary, and your role at this point in this journey is crucial. As the world around us revolves and evolves, so does the ONE|Local app. — transforming into a vibrant social coordination platform that will empower us to deepen our engagement in the community we cherish. a testament to our collective growth and the maturation of our co-created vision. This upgrade will, yes, bless us with a new look, but it is so much more than that.
ONE|Boulder has been the seed, and will remain among the many branches on the ONE|Local olive tree. Yes, we are growing. And with the expansion, we are also enhancing our collective ability to manage projects, events, and organizations, and broadening our cultural horizon to embrace more family-oriented events. We are here for integration and collective maturation. For the diversity of experiences and opportunities that are necessary for a whole and well ecosystem of humans to thrive in body, heart and soul.

We want to be in a shared context where the world makes sense, where we hold roles we’re proud of, where we share our ways, and feel welcomed and at home in the world.
Imagine a world where every contribution is valued, where every skill and resource can find its perfect match. That's the future we're building with ONE|Local. Whether it’s sharing a skill, organizing an event, or connecting over shared values, the new ONE|Local app is designed to make these exchanges seamless and rewarding.
We’re introducing features that allow you to create and manage organizations, groups, projects, roles, and tasks with ease, ensuring that you can find or offer the perfect skill or resource for any need. This means more visibility for your events, more support for your projects, and a stronger community fabric woven through shared purpose and mutual support.
This is more than just an app.
ONE|Local is a movement towards a world where every interaction enriches our community, where every exchange strengthens our bonds, where every connection increases our capacity to love and be loved.
