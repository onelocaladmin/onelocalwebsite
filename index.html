About ONE [Local]

ONE Local Foundation is organization dedicated to empowering communities and improving access to resources for underserved individuals and families. We develop software solutions that address community needs and partner with organizations that share our mission.

Through collaborative development and deployment, we aim to create impactful and sustainable solutions. Our software is tailored to the specific challenges faced by communities, helping them streamline processes, enhance communication, and maximize their impact.

If your organization is aligned with our mission, we invite you to explore partnership opportunities and join us in making a difference.
