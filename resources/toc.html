Nurturing the Tree of Community in the Rising Tide

Introduction: The Metaphor of the Tree

In a storm-tossed sea of the contemporary mythic flood, a tree rises, its heartbeat sending waves of peace into the surrounding ocean, defying the surging tides. This tree is a beacon of hope, resilience, and interconnectedness, standing tall with roots entwined in the human spirit and ecological balance, offering shelter to all who seek it in a world threatened by disconnection.

ONE Local: A Member-Governed Platform

ONE Local is a member-governed platform for social coordination, with voting members of various classes who can offer or request skills and services, send "gratis" points, create projects and events, and delineate groups, roles, and tasks. The goal is streamlined intent matching, relating our work to a shared mythic purpose, and orienting ourselves as one human family.

Our Mission and Values

We are agents optimizing culture and civilization for the most gratifying experience possible, balanced with healthy ecosystems and creative innovation. As crises converge, we must recohere a life-affirming shared meaning, acknowledging our interconnectedness and moving beyond self-serving ambition.

The Role of AI and Human Experience

As AI colonizes every field, the last fertile ground is tending to human needs and experiences - sharing unique ways and wares, owning our information, and living well together on earth.

Introducing ONE|Local

ONE|Local is a vibrant platform empowering deeper community engagement, collective growth, and cultural interconnectedness. It showcases existing cultures, holds space for new ones, and fosters a world where every contribution is valued, and every skill and resource finds its perfect match.

Governance and Co-creation

ONE|Local is a movement towards enriching interactions, strengthened bonds, and increased capacity for love. Through collaboration and genuine cooperation, we set a precedent for thriving communities worldwide. Your feedback and needs shape the app and tools we build.

A Vision of Economic Revolution and Local Empowerment

ONE|Local ignites an economic revolution, putting power back in the hands of the people. By sourcing locally, we increase resilience, compound community wealth, and stake more into our homeland. Our goal is to disintermediate the gap between resources and needs in every locality.

Civic Engagement and Technology's Role

We are part of an emerging civic tech ecology, with partners like People, Polly, Ethelo, and Polity, building tools for empowered engagement and revolutionizing citizen-data interaction. We support the Independent National Convention and the growing Independent Movement. Civic technology that processes collective decision-making data is mission-critical for evolving engagement and preventing global conflict.

Reconnecting to Our Roots and Community Needs

Our mission is to connect people with true nourishment, being there in hard times, and stepping into a paradigm of honed skills and open hearts. We support local families, farms, ecological health, and vibrant hometown culture. It's time to re-cohere what is civil, reclaim our identities as agents of society, and realize an alternative socioeconomic order that minimizes precarity, maximizes the middle class, and rewards innovation.

Conclusion: A Call to Action

Join us in weaving the engram of cultural interconnectedness, celebrating the rich tapestry of human existence, and stepping into a future where every individual finds belonging, meaning, and joy. Together, we navigate the journey of life, sharing wisdom and experiences, bound by the deep coherence of community, culture, and connection to the natural world.
